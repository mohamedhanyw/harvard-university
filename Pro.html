Location of Harvard University

Harvard University is located in Cambridge, Massachusetts, USA. The university campus is situated along the Charles River, which separates Cambridge from the city of Boston. The campus is divided into several areas, including the historic Harvard Yard, which contains many of the university's original buildings, as well as modern academic and research facilities.

Cambridge is a vibrant and diverse city that is home to many other universities and cultural institutions. It is known for its lively arts and music scene, as well as its many restaurants, cafes, and shops. The city is easily accessible by public transportation, and is just a short distance from Boston's Logan International Airport.
